About

I'm a final-year Business Engineering student at Solvay Brussels School, majoring in Finance, with an exchange semester in MSc Finance at Tilburg University. My work sits where rigorous quantitative methods meet real investment judgment.

Across coursework and projects I've built full DCF, LBO and M&A models, priced exotic derivatives with Monte Carlo simulation, and tested asset-pricing theory on decades of US equity data.

Equity Research

WD-40 Company Valuation

Solo report · 2025

Full intrinsic valuation of WD-40 using a WACC-based DCF (4.1%) with a Fama-French cost of assets. Issued a Buy on a $252.98 intrinsic value vs. $216.04 market - ~17% upside - backed by a WACC/terminal-growth sensitivity grid.

Derivatives Valuation - NVIDIA

Solo project · Tilburg

Built a 10,000-path Monte Carlo engine in Python to price vanilla and exotic (Asian, Lookback) options on NVDA, benchmarked against Black-Scholes for convergence, and constructed a delta-gamma hedged portfolio.

Empirical Research

CAPM & Fama-French on 75 US Equities

Solo project · 1987-2025

Tested the CAPM and a three-factor model across a stratified sample of 75 US stocks: estimated the Security Market Line, ran cross-sectional regressions, built mean-variance frontiers, and analysed size/value effects and survivorship bias.
